Recipe for Delicious Pizza

A mouth-watering pepperoni pizza

Make the best homemade pepperoni pizza recipe with this step by step guide.
Use either a pizza stone or a baking sheet and have it on the table in under 30 minutes!
Let's make a classic cheese and pepperoni pizza...

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Stretch pizza dough
  2. Add pizza dough to lightly oiled pizza stone
  3. Add pizza sauce
  4. Add toppings (mozzarella cheese, pepperoni slices, parmesan cheese)
  5. Bake pizza 10-15 minutes
  6. Top with fresh parmesan and basil
